About Sovereign Africa Ratings

Sovereign Africa Ratings (SAR) is a licensed provider of credit rating services and research. We are driven by the principles of efficient operations and world-class technical competencies. SAR was formed in order to satisfy developmental and investment needs in emerging markets. SAR is an organisation that is committed to excellence in the provision of credit rating services to its clients throughout the continent and the developing world. SAR asserts that it is clear that the needs of emerging markets are unique, and that the length of time it has taken for economic transformation to be realised on a large scale indicates the need for more plurality in the provision of quality research, objective creditworthiness opinions, and linked market insights.
